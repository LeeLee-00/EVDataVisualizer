Data Overview

The "Electric_Vehicle_Population_Data.csv" file contains a dataset with the following characteristics:


  1. VIN (1-10): Partial Vehicle Identification Number
  2. County: County where the vehicle is registered
  3. City: County where the vehicle is registered
  4. State: County where the vehicle is registered
  5. Postal Code: County where the vehicle is registered
  6. Model Year: County where the vehicle is registered
  7. Make: County where the vehicle is registered
  8. Model: County where the vehicle is registered
  9. Electric Vehicle Type: County where the vehicle is registered
  10. CAFV Eligibility: County where the vehicle is registered
  11. Electric Range:County where the vehicle is registered
  12. Base MSRP: Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price
  13. Legislative District: Legislative district for the vehicle location
  14. DOL Vehicle ID: Department of Licensing Vehicle ID
  15. GEOCODED COLUMN: Cordinates
  16. Vehicle Location: Geographical location coordinates
  17. Electric Utility: Electric utility provider in the area
  18. 2020 Census Tract: Census tract number
